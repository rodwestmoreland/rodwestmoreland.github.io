Core Values

excellence in all we do

Some might recognize the bullets above as the US Air Force Core Values. A keen observer would notice that I spent most of my military career with the US Navy. However, I did begin as an airman and those original values shaped who I was and who I strived to be. To me, they describe the values of the Navy - Honor, Courage, Commitment - in a more palpable way that echoes in my mind in daily interactions. It does astound me reflecting on how these short statements from both great institutions have impacted me over the years. The desire to live up to those values are a constant motivator for me. Not to say that I have never failed miserably at achieving those standards at times, but it is always a constant reminder of the kind of person I need to be.

Be above reproach. Be the one who drives the mission forward, regardless of personal desires. Be the one who works with others to make the organization the absolute best that it can be.
